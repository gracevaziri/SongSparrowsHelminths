This file describes the variables in 
float_data.csv
Which was used in 
Anthelmintic drugs modulate the acute phase immune response but not the microbiota in wild Song Sparrows
"by Grace J. Vaziri, Michelle A. Jusino, Jon A. Palmer, Matthew T. Brewer and James S. Adelman"

Variable and descriptions: 
bird_id = band number given to a bird
pre/post = whether the sample was collected before or after a bird was treated with anthelmintic drugs
parasite = whether parasite eggs were detected (and what kind) or not (npd = no parasites detected)
Anti-helm.. = whether bird was treated with anthelmintic drug (Y) or water control (N)
